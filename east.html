East Himalaya

Starting in Calcutta, the rally will take you North to the former kingdom of Sikkim driving through the tea plantations of Darjeeling, before heading to Bhutan, the land of Happiness. South to the Indian state of Assam crossing the border into Bangladesh.

Rally Dates - 5 December to 29 December
Rally Distance - 3.244km
Start - Calcutta
End - Calcutta
Number Of Cars - Limited to 25 cars
