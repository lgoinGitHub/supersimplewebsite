Colin McRae

Birthdate: August 5, 1968
Sun Sign: Leo
Birthplace: Lanark
Join Club Date: March 20, 2001

Colin McRae was a British rally driver who won the British Rally Championship title in 2001 and 2002. In 2005, he became the youngest person and the first British driver to win the World Rally Championship Drivers' title. Colin McRae's career was cut short by his sudden demise in 2007 when he was killed in a helicopter crash.

Jenson Button is a British racing driver best known for winning the Rally Championship title in 2009. The only Formula rally Champion from Africa, Jody David Scheckter made his debut at the US Grand Prix in 2000, shortly after moving from South Africa to Britain.
